FAQ

Questions people might ask

What can SmartFind AI help me find?
SmartFind AI can help you locate products, services, tools, software, learning resources, and general information — then present curated options with direct links.
Is this giving legal, medical, or financial advice?
No. SmartFind AI does not provide legal, medical, or financial advice. It may suggest general information or point you toward professionals, but it is not a substitute for licensed experts.
How many questions do I get per session?
Each paid session includes up to 5 questions. You can use them all at once or as follow‑ups within the same session.
How is my payment handled?
Payments are processed securely through a third‑party payment processor. Your card details are never stored on this site.
Who controls this service and the revenue?
This service is operated and controlled by a single owner. Only the owner has access to the admin dashboard, logs, and revenue, which is routed to a bank account of their choosing.
What about copyrighted content?
If you paste or upload content that appears to be copyrighted, SmartFind AI will not reproduce or redistribute it. It may summarize or discuss it at a high level and may flag the interaction for review.
